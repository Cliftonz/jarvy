# Gitpod is now Ona: your AI software engineer

Rebrand announcement. Re-founding the company as Ona — "mission control for your personal team of software engineering agents."

Architecture pillars:
1. **Ona Environments** — API-first sandboxed cloud dev envs. Declarative via devcontainer.json + automations.yml. Runs in Ona cloud OR customer infra.
2. **Ona Agents** — Personal team of SWE agents. Private LLM access + MCP. Slash commands for codified workflows (e.g. /review-like-mads).
3. **Ona Guardrails** — Enterprise tier. SDK/API access. Bank-grade VPC, RBAC, SSO/OIDC, command deny lists, full audit trail. Models via Bedrock, Vertex, private APIs.

Internal proof: Ona co-authored 60% of PRs merged on main and 72% of LOC merged in their own product engineering org. Customers report 4x throughput.

Scale claim: 2 million developers used Gitpod. Quadrupled Enterprise ARR YoY. "Near 8-figure contract" recently signed. Customers include America's largest banks, Europe's leading financial + pharma, Asia's top sovereign wealth funds.

Gitpod history: Originally "one-click online IDE for GitHub" → CDEs → Ona (AI agent platform).
